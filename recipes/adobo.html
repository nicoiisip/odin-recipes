Chicken Adobo (Adobong Manok)

Chicken adobo is a classic Filipino dish that's as savory as it is bright with acid, and it goes perfectly with a large platter of garlic fried rice.

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 lb chicken thighs bone in
  • 4 cloves garlic crushed
  • 3 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • â…” cup white vinegar
  • 1 tbsp light brown sugar
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 tsp black peppercorns

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a big pot. I used a Dutch oven. Add the garlic and chicken thighs, skin side down. Sear the chicken on both sides.
  2. Once done searing the chicken, add the oyster sauce and soy sauce. Mix to the coat the chicken.
  3. Add the vinegar, brown sugar, bay leaves, and peppercorns. Bring the liquid to a boil and lower to a simmer.
  4. Simmer on low heat for 1 hour. Flip the chicken every 20 minutes, so each side gets an even amount of color.
  5. Serve chicken over a bed of garlic rice.
